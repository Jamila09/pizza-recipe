Delicious Pizza Recipe

This homemade pizza recipe is easy to follow and results in a delicious, cheesy pizza that you can customize with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, salt, and olive oil.
  3. Gradually add the water and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Add the sliced pepperoni, bell peppers, and mushrooms.
  11. Season with oregano, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  13. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
